Key Words and Concept:

  1. Add:  to put things or numbers together

  2. Cause: make something happen 

  3. Contact: when two things touch each other; meeting or writing to people

  4. Explain: show, tell, etc. what something means

  5. Vocabulary: all the words in a language 

  6. Enter: to go or come into a place

 
 

 Instructional Objectives:

1. Learning some new words such as add, cause, contact, explain, vocabulary, enter, etc.

2. Knowing about the ways languages change

How Languages Change

Read the following passage as carefully and as fast as you can. You can also listen to a recording of this passage after doing the comprehension check.

No one knows all the reasons why languages change, but they continue to do so as long as people speak them. In a few cases, the changes can be explained. For example, words are added to a vocabulary to refer to new ideas or objects. Contacts between speakers of different languages may cause words from one language to enter another language.
